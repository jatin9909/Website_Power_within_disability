OVERVIEW

The course is designed to aim at imparting a basic level appreciation programme for the common man. After completing the course the incubent will be able to use the computer for basic purposes of preparing his personnel/business letters, viewing information on internet(the web), sending mails etc. The course provides a good working environment with a friendly interface. The course is scheduled on weekly basis with 28 video lectures and one final assesment per week.
