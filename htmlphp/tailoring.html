OVERVIEW

Tailoring is one of the oldest of the textile arts, and is the craft of fastening or attaching objects using stitches made with a needle and thread.Garment construction is usually guided by a pattern. Our course stitching machine operation aims at producing excellent tailoring masters who excell in the skills of interlining, seaming, garmenting diffent clothes.The course provides a good working environment with a friendly interface. The course is scheduled on weekly basis with 14 video lectures and one final assesment per week.
