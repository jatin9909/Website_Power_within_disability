OVERVIEW

With a booming radio industry, a career in this field has of late become very lucrative and promising.The course content will cover pronunciation, vocal exercises, news reading, microphone rehersals, production techniques and editing. The course will not only help to build a better career option but also as something which will strengthen their communication skills and develop their personalities.
